What's this about?


Intro

Hello and welcome to Armchair Mage Dev. My name is Valmitic and I've been playing World of Warcraft for over 12 years now. I started in August 2007 with the first class being Mage. Throughout my years of playing, I've switched different classes for a short time for each different raid tier for each expansion but always switch back to my mage.


The Synopsis

After the Legion expansion. Mages (and most classes really) lost not only their artifact spell, but a lot of passives from their artifact weapons while gaining next to nothing in return. I created this website with the intent to fixing some of the problems with the mage class right now in Battle for Azeroth. The artifact abilties that where made talents in BfA are now part of the specialization (like Ebonbolt), and some of the passives effects from the artifacts and legiondaries have been baked into each specialization or is now a talent in the talent tree.


A Disclaimer

Before you scroll down further, the levels shown on the talent tree are based off leaks regarding the upcoming expansion for WoW with the possibility of a level squish. And any other information for the next expansion that was posted on 4chan, then MMO champion and Reddit. Most of the numbers you see on the new spells are not 100% set in stone.

General Talents Changes


    Level 20 (30 pre xpac) talent changes


  • Shimmer: Recharge reduced by 2 sec and Tooltip update - No longer breaks stuns and roots

  • Talent One and Three: The First and Third talents in this row has been replaced or redesign for each specalization. See below for more details.

    Level 30 (45 pre xpac) talent changes


  • Incanter's Flow now has an active component to it.

  • Mirror Image has recieved an update. Each Mirror Image now gains a 2% damage buff, stacking up to 5 times (10% damage for each Mirror Image).

  • Rune of Power radius increased up to 15 yards. Now reapears underneath the caster's feet whenever the caster casts a damage spell outside of Rune of Power.

    Level 45 (75 pre xpac) talent changes


  • Ice Ward and Ring of Frost are now Frost only talents. Arcane and Fire has recieved their own version along with an update to their functions.

Notes and Clarifications

  • Mark of the Magi is basically Mark of Aluneth from the legion artifact weapon with the same name

  • Supernova's slow decays by 10% every sec

  • Time Anomaly has a 50% of activating one or the other.

  • Activating Alternate Reality can reset the cooldown of Presence of Mind. Arcane Blast will still function like the mage is casting it at 4 stacks of Arcane Charge.

  • The proposed version of Prismatic Crystal has changed to where the mage now has to target an enemy in order to activate it, whereas before it would spawn where the cursor is pointing at and sit there. If the target dies while Prismatic Crystal is still active, it will sit there until the mage attacks a new target, teleporting and latching on to its new target.
